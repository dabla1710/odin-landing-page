Thor is the Norse god of thunder, known for his immense strength and his powerful hammer, Mjolnir.
Thor protects the gods and humans from the giants and other threats.
He is seen as a champion of order and justice.

These are some of the closest companions Thor has:

Odin is Thor's father, the Allfather of the Norse gods.

Freya played a crucial role in helping Thor retrieve his hammer, Mjolnir, from the giants.

Loki is Thor's adoptive brother and frequent nemesis, often causing mischief and trouble for him.

Tyr is Thor's half-brother, both being sons of Odin.
